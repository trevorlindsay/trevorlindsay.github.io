Hello, My Name is Trevor.

Born and raised in Stone Mountain, Georgia. Curious and ambitious data scientist with a knack for solving unstructured and complicated problems.

I currently work at Machine Zone, where I spend most of my time conducting really cool exploratory analyses on our massive data sets with the goal of better understanding user behavior. I also recently accepted a Machine Learning Fellowship with Startup.ML; I'll be researching and applying advanced methods in deep learning to develop finance products.

I graduated from Georgia Tech in December 2014 with a BS in Economics. My coursework and research focused on applied microeconomics and my honors thesis was titled, Merger Enforcement and Innovation.

After graduation, I went to work in investment banking at SunTrust Robinson Humphrey. While working late one evening, I had an ah-ha moment and realized that I was wasting my time doing something I really didn't like. The next week, I quit my job and pivoted towards my true passion, solving problems with data. And now, here I am!
