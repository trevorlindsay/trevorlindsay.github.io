Data Scientist, Facebook

Hello, My Name is Trevor.

Born and raised in Stone Mountain, Georgia. Curious and ambitious product guy with a knack for solving unstructured and complicated problems.

I currently work as a data scientist on the Marketplace Integrity team at Facebook. My team's goal is to enable people to make confident commerce decisions on Facebook. I'm driving the insights, experimental design, measurement and analyses instrumental to keeping Facebook and Marketplace a safe place to transact.

I am also the lead instructor for General Assembly's part-time data science program. Across two 3-hour classes a week for 10 weeks, I teach students common data science techniques with a focus on exploratory data analysis, statistics and predictive modeling.

Before Facebook, I was a Live Operations Manager at Machine Zone (the gaming company befind Game of War: Fire Age and Mobile Strike). While there, I spent most of my time analyzing user data and building models of user behavior to improve monetization and retention.

I graduated with honors from Georgia Tech in 2014 with a BS in Economics. My coursework focused on applied microeconomics and my honors thesis was titled The Law and Economics of Merger Enforcement and Innovation.
