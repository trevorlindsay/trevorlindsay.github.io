Hello, My Name is Trevor.

Born and raised in Stone Mountain, Georgia. Curious and ambitious machine learning engineer with a knack for solving unstructured and complicated problems with code.

I currently work at Machine Zone (the real-time technology company behind Game of War: Fire Age and Mobile Strike) where I spend most of my time building really cool models of user behavior. I am also finishing up a 4-month fellowing with Startup.ML where I am building machine learning systems and helping startups solve interesting problems in finance.

I graduated from Georgia Tech in December 2014 with a BS in Economics. My coursework and research focused on applied microeconomics and my honors thesis was titled, Merger Enforcement and Innovation (I recently started my MS in Computer Science at Georgia Tech as well, through their new online masters program).

After undergrad, I went to work in investment banking. While working late one evening, I had an ah-ha moment and realized that I was wasting my time doing something I really didn't like. The next week, I quit my job and pivoted towards my true passion -- building intelligent systems. And now, here I am!
